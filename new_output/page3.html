Post #3. Siamese

The Siamese cat is one of the first distinctly recognised breeds of Asian cat.


It derives from the Wichianmat landrace. The Siamese cat is one of several varieties of cats native to Thailand (known as Siam before 1939). The original Siamese became one of the most popular breeds in Europe and North America in the 19th century.

Post #2. Maine Coon

The Maine Coon is a large domesticated cat breed.


One of the oldest natural breeds in North America, it originated in the U.S. state of Maine, where it is the official state cat. The Maine Coon is a large and social cat, commonly referred to as "the gentle giant."
