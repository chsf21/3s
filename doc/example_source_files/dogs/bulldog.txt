The Bulldog is a British breed of dog of mastiff type.
(IMAGE bulldog.jpg)
It may also be known as the English Bulldog or British Bulldog. It is a stocky, muscular dog of medium size, with a large head, thick folds of skin around the face and shoulders and a relatively flat face with a protruding lower jaw.
