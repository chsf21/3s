The Maine Coon is a large domesticated cat breed.
(IMAGE maine_coon.png)
One of the oldest natural breeds in North America, it originated in the U.S. state of Maine, where it is the official state cat. The Maine Coon is a large and social cat, commonly referred to as "the gentle giant."
